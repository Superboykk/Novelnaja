# Introduction: The Awakening

It was a dark and stormy night... The kingdom of Eldoria was silent, but in the shadows, something was stirring.

The ancient runes had begun to glow with an eerie violet light, a sign that the seal of the Shadow King was weakening. High Priest Alistair stood at the observatory tower, his hands trembling as he stared at the sky. A blood-red star was rising in the east.

"The prophecy," he whispered. "It is happening."

For three thousand years, the land had known peace. The tales of the Shadow War had become nothing more than fairy tales to frighten young children before bed. But Alistair knew the truth. He had spent his entire life guarding the ancient archives.

He knew that when the blood-red star rose, a hero of light must emerge from the valley, or the kingdom would fall into eternal darkness.
